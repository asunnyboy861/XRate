Privacy Policy for XRate

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

XRate is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our currency conversion service.

Data you provide:

  • Feedback messages and email address (only when you choose to contact support)

Data collected automatically:

  • No personal data is collected automatically
  • No tracking identifiers or advertising identifiers are used
  • No analytics data is collected

2. How We Use Information

The only information we process is:

  • Feedback messages you voluntarily send through the Contact Support feature
  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry

3. Data Storage

XRate stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Cached exchange rates for offline use
  • Your preferred currency selections
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

XRate uses the following third-party services:

  • Frankfurter API (api.frankfurter.dev): Used to fetch exchange rate data. No personal information is sent to this service.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used for in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

XRate does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any data collection

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
