FAQ

How often are exchange rates updated?

Exchange rates are updated every 30 minutes when you have an internet connection. Cached rates are available offline.

Why is the rate different from other sources?

XRate uses the Frankfurter API which sources data from the European Central Bank. Rates may differ slightly from other providers due to different data sources and update frequencies.

How do I add more currencies?

Tap the currency code button (e.g., "USD") to open the currency picker. You can search by currency code, name, or country.

Does XRate work offline?

Yes! XRate caches the last fetched exchange rates so you can perform conversions even without internet. You'll see an offline indicator when using cached data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, or directly in the App Store.
