## App Description

XRate is the minimalist currency converter designed for speed and simplicity. Whether you're traveling abroad, shopping internationally, or managing global finances — XRate gives you instant, accurate conversions at a glance.

KEY FEATURES:

• Instant Conversion — Real-time exchange rates for 40+ world currencies
• Offline Mode — Cached rates work even without internet
• Trend Charts — Visualize 7-day to 1-year exchange rate trends
• Quick Access — One-tap access to popular currencies
• Smart Search — Find currencies by code, name, or country
• Swap Currencies — Instantly reverse your conversion with one tap
• Beautiful Design — Clean, modern interface following Apple's design guidelines

PRO FEATURES:

• Unlimited currency conversions
• Historical rate charts (up to 1 year)
• Desktop Widget
• Apple Watch App
• Batch conversion
• Travel mode with location-based suggestions
• Custom decimal places

XRate uses reliable data from the European Central Bank via the Frankfurter API, ensuring accurate and up-to-date exchange rates. No sign-up required — just open and convert.

Download XRate today and make currency conversion effortless!
